About Proof of Humanity

Learn about our mission to create a trusted network of verified humans through a unique web of trust approach.

Proof of Humanity is a revolutionary system designed to verify human identity through trusted social connections rather than relying on centralized authorities or invasive identity verification methods.

Our invitation-only network creates a web of trust where each member is personally verified by existing members, ensuring a high level of trust and protection against fraudulent accounts and sybil attacks.

By focusing on human relationships as the foundation of verification, we enable a wide range of applications that require trusted identity verification while protecting privacy and personal data.

Web of Trust Verification

Our unique approach uses social connections and personal vouching to verify identity, creating a strong network of trust without central authorities.

Privacy-Preserving

We use advanced privacy technologies to ensure that verification happens without exposing personal data to third parties.

Sybil-Resistant

Our multi-layered verification process makes it extremely difficult for individuals to create multiple fake identities within the network.
