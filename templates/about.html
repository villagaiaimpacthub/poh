About Proof of Humanity

Learn about our mission and the technology behind our human verification network

Our Mission

Proof of Humanity is a revolutionary system designed to verify human identity in the digital world. By creating a decentralized registry of unique human identities, we aim to solve one of the most challenging problems of the digital age - proving that a digital user is indeed a unique human being.

Our mission is to build a more trustworthy and fair digital environment where verified humans can interact with confidence.

Our Technology

We use decentralized identity technology to ensure users maintain control of their personal data while still providing proof of their humanity.

Our unique verification system builds a network of trust through family trees, where each verified member can vouch for new members, creating a robust web of verified humans.

Encrypted verification calls ensure secure and private identity confirmation while protecting personal information.

How It Works

  1. Join the Network - Create an account and connect with existing members who can vouch for your humanity.
  2. Complete Verification - Participate in verification calls with existing members who will confirm your unique identity.
  3. Grow the Network - Once verified, help verify new members and build your family tree.
  4. Access Benefits - Enjoy the benefits of a verified identity, including exclusive access to services.
