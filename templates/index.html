Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Proof of Humanity

What is Proof of Humanity?

Proof of Humanity is a novel approach to verifying human identities in digital spaces. Unlike traditional methods that rely on personal documentation or centralized authorities, we leverage trusted family relationships to create a robust and privacy-preserving verification system.

How does family verification work?

Users create a family tree by inviting trusted family members. Through a simple video verification process, family members confirm each other's identities. Once a user has been verified by enough family members, they receive a cryptographic proof of their humanity that can be used across web3 applications.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes. Proof of Humanity is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal personal information, and the verification process happens between you and your family members, not through a centralized authority. Your data is encrypted and you control who has access to your verification status.

What if I don't have family members to verify me?

While family verification is our primary method, we understand not everyone has family members who can participate. In such cases, we offer alternative verification paths through trusted community members or established verification authorities. Contact our support team for assistance.

Where can I use my Proof of Humanity verification?

Your Proof of Humanity verification can be used across any web3 application or service that integrates with our protocol. We're actively partnering with platforms across DeFi, NFT marketplaces, voting systems, and other web3 services to expand the utility of your verification.
